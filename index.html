 About

Dial n Dine is a national call centre geared to meet the online/telephonic ordering and customer-engagement needs of retail food both on delivery and on collection.The main focus of the call centre is to get and keep customers supporting the business and increasing growth in sales. However the call centre does not focus only on customers but also it relieves the pressure from different stores that offer delivery/collection on a day to day basis. This particular call centre takes calls for Debonairs, Steers and Fishaways. These are all franchises of Famous Brands
